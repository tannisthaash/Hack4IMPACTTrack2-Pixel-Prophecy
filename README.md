**PROBLEM STATEMENT** - Large amounts of surplus food from restaurants and events are wasted daily due to lack of proper redistribution systems.
At the same time, NGOs and underprivileged communities struggle to access fresh and sufficient food.
There is no real-time platform to efficiently connect food donors with the right recipients.
Logistical challenges like distance, coordination, and food safety further limit timely delivery.
